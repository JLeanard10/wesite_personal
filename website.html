Projects

"Automated Inventory Management System for Small Businesses"

Efficient inventory management is crucial for the success of small businesses. However, many small businesses struggle with manual inventory tracking, leading to stock shortages, overstocking, and financial loss. This project proposes the development of an Automated Inventory Management System that uses real-time tracking and predictive analytics to manage inventory more effectively, ensuring that stock levels are always optimized.

"Online Voting System with Blockchain Technology"

With the rise of digital technologies, there is a growing demand for secure and transparent online voting systems. This project proposes the development of an Online Voting System that leverages blockchain technology to ensure the integrity, transparency, and security of votes, enabling people to vote remotely while preventing fraud and manipulation.

"Real-Time Health Monitoring System Using Wearable Technology"

Advances in wearable technology have opened up new possibilities for real-time health monitoring. This project proposes the development of a Real-Time Health Monitoring System that utilizes wearable devices to track vital signs (e.g., heart rate, body temperature, and oxygen levels) and provides real-time updates to both users and healthcare providers. This system will help in early detection of health anomalies, enabling timely medical intervention.
